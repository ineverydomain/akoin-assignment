About IDR

The Institute for Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to cultivating talent for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. We focus on preparing professionals to thrive in regulated, high-consequence environments where precision and expertise are paramount.

By bridging the gap between academic insight—through our UK university partnerships—and real-world industry projects, we ensure our practitioners are equipped with both theoretical foundations and practical, hands-on experience.
